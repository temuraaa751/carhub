Overview

The 2024 Toyota RAV4 combines versatility, efficiency, and style in one package. Whether you’re commuting through the city or exploring the great outdoors, the RAV4 offers an impressive hybrid option, advanced safety features, and modern technology to suit every adventure.

Key Features

  • Performance: Available in gas, hybrid, and plug-in hybrid options.
  • Technology: Toyota's latest multimedia system with a 10.5-inch touchscreen and Apple CarPlay/Android Auto.
  • Safety: Toyota Safety Sense™ 3.0 with advanced driver-assist features.
  • Comfort: Spacious interior with up to 69.8 cubic feet of cargo space.
  • Trim Options: LE, XLE, XSE, Limited, Adventure, and TRD Off-Road models.

The History of Toyota RAV4

Introduced in 1994, the Toyota RAV4 was the world's first compact crossover SUV, combining the capabilities of an SUV with the drivability of a sedan. Over the years, the RAV4 has set industry standards for efficiency and innovation, consistently ranking among the best-selling SUVs globally.

With its evolution into the 2024 model, the RAV4 continues to push boundaries with a focus on sustainability, including hybrid and plug-in hybrid powertrains, while still delivering the reliability Toyota is known for.
