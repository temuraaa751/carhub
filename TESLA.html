About the Tesla Model S Plaid

The Tesla Model S Plaid, introduced in 2021, is the pinnacle of Tesla's engineering and design. With an insane 1,020 horsepower and a record-breaking 0-60 mph acceleration of under 2 seconds, the Plaid is the fastest production sedan in history. Combining cutting-edge technology, unparalleled performance, and luxurious comfort, it truly sets the standard for electric vehicles.

Key Features

  • Performance: A tri-motor all-wheel drive system delivering 1,020 horsepower.
  • Range: Up to 396 miles on a single charge.
  • Technology: Tesla's Autopilot for assisted driving, a 17-inch cinematic display, and over-the-air updates.
  • Interior: Minimalist design with a yoke steering wheel and premium materials.
  • Charging: Supercharging compatibility for fast and convenient charging worldwide.
